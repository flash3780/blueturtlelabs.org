Blue Turtle Labs is currently working on several exciting engineering projects.

Stirling Engine Design

We are currently working on the design of a commercially viable stirling engine, which will ultimately provide an integrated power solution for solar and wind-powered generation systems. This technology will provide economical off-grid power solutions for rural applications.

Post Anchor Design

We are currently working on an anchoring system to connect wooden posts to concrete footings. This anchor design provides a strong support for wooden railings and fences. Key features of this anchor design include:

  • Able to attach pressure treated wooden posts to existing or new concrete footings. Attachment shall withstand significant loading, making it suitable for a wide variety of applications.
  • Anchoring system allows for wooden posts to be replaced in case of rot or decay without damaging concrete footing.
